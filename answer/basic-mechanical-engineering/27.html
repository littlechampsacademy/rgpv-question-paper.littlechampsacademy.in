Q.27: Describe the expression for thermal efficiency of Otto cycle. Calculate the value for a compression ratio of 8.

Answer:
Thermal efficiency

Since the processes 1-2 and 3-4 are adiabatic, no heat transfer takes place during these processes. The heat transfer is limited to addition of heat during the constant volume process and rejection of heat during constant volume process 4-1.
