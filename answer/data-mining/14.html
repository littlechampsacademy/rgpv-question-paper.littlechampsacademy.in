Q.14 How can we study time-series data ?
Or
What is the time series analysis ? What are the various detected patterns in time series ?

Ans. In general, there are two goals in time-series analysis – modeling time series and forecasting time series, Trend analysis consists of the following four major components or movements for characterizing time-series data –
Major components for trend analysis in time series data
i. Trend or Long term Movements: Indicates the general direction in which a time series is moving over long or short interval of time through trend curve or trend line.
ii. Cyclic Movement or Cyclic Variations: Long term oscillations about a trend curve or line which may or may not be periodic.
iii. Seasonal Movements or Variations: These are systematic or calendar related.
Eg. Sudden rise in sales of sweets in Tihar.
iv. Irregular or Random Movements: Series due to random or chance events.
Eg. Price rise in crisis of supply.
